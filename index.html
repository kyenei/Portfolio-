Hello! I'm Quien Garnet R. Cruz, a 3rd year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at STI College Tanay. My forte lies in UI Design — I love creating interfaces that are not just functional, but visually thoughtful and user-centered.

My journey in technology started during Senior High School under the Mobile App and Web Development strand, where I discovered my passion for design and development. Now in college, I continue to grow and explore new ideas through every project I take on.

Beyond the screen, I'm also a filmmaker and storyteller. I believe creativity in any form — whether through code, design, or cinema — is always about connecting with people in a meaningful way.

Jolly Dough Restaurant System

A restaurant ordering system for a pastry and drinks shop offering Donuts, Cookies, Cupcakes, Coffee, Frappe, and Fruit Shakes. The system handles customer orders, displays prices, and computes total costs — eliminating the need to queue at the counter or have staff manually take orders.

Research / System
Attendance Management System (AMS)

A proposal and validation of a QR Code-based Attendance Management System for teachers at STI College Tanay (A.Y. 2022–2023). The system streamlines student attendance recording, monitors tardiness patterns, and automatically flags students with three consecutive late arrivals — benefiting teachers, students, and parents alike.
